# Terms & Conditions

**Effective Date:** July 19, 2026

## Overview

These Terms & Conditions govern the use of **Andrew Mack Uptime Alerts**, a private personal SMS notification service described on this website.

The service delivers automated infrastructure monitoring alerts generated by Uptime Kuma and is intended solely for personal operational monitoring. It is not used for marketing, advertising, promotional messaging, or public customer communications.

---

## Types of Messages

Authorized recipients may receive automated SMS notifications including:

- Server availability alerts
- Service outage notifications
- Home Internet connectivity alerts
- CCTV connectivity notifications
- Infrastructure health and operational alerts
- Service recovery notifications

Messages are generated automatically when monitored events or status changes occur.

---

## Recipient Consent

Recipients are added only after explicitly agreeing to receive automated SMS notifications from Andrew Mack Uptime Alerts.

Before a phone number is added, the recipient is informed about:

- The nature of the monitoring notifications
- Variable message frequency
- Possible message and data charges
- The ability to reply **STOP** to unsubscribe
- The ability to reply **HELP** for assistance

The exact consent language and process are available on the [SMS Consent page](consent).

This service does not offer public registration, subscriptions, mailing lists, or marketing programs.

---

## Message Frequency

Message frequency varies depending on monitored system events.

Recipients may receive multiple messages during service interruptions, outages, maintenance events, status changes, or recovery events.

## Opt-Out

Recipients may opt out of SMS notifications at any time by replying **STOP** to any message.

Recipients may also request removal directly from the account owner.

After opting out, no further monitoring notifications will be sent unless the recipient later requests to resubscribe.
