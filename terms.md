---

## Types of Messages

Recipients may receive automated SMS notifications including, but not limited to:

- Server availability alerts
- Service outage notifications
- Home Internet connectivity alerts
- CCTV monitoring notifications
- Infrastructure health and operational alerts

Messages are generated automatically when monitored events occur.

Recipients are added only after providing their consent to receive SMS notifications.

Message frequency varies depending on monitored system events.
