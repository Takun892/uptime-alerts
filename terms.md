# Terms & Conditions

This SMS notification service provides automated alerts generated by personal infrastructure monitoring.

## Message Types

Messages may include:

- Server outage notifications
- Service availability alerts
- Home Internet outage alerts
- CCTV connectivity notifications

## Message Frequency

Message frequency varies depending on monitoring events.

## Opt-Out

Recipients may reply STOP at any time to stop receiving SMS notifications.

## Help

Recipients may reply HELP for assistance.

## Carrier Notice

Message and data rates may apply.

Carrier delivery is not guaranteed.
