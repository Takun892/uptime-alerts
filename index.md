# Personal Infrastructure Monitoring

Welcome to the Personal Infrastructure Monitoring information site.

This website provides information regarding a private SMS notification service used to monitor personal infrastructure and network availability.

Automated notifications are generated by **Uptime Kuma** and delivered only to authorized recipients who have provided consent to receive operational monitoring alerts.

This service is intended solely for personal infrastructure monitoring and is **not** used for marketing, advertising, promotional messaging, or customer communications.

---

## Notification Types

Recipients may receive notifications regarding:

- Server availability
- Service outages
- Home Internet connectivity
- CCTV monitoring
- Infrastructure health
- Service recovery notifications

Message frequency varies depending on monitored system events.

---

## Recipient Eligibility

SMS notifications are sent only to authorized recipients who have explicitly agreed to receive monitoring alerts.

No public registration, subscription, mailing list, or marketing program exists for this service.
