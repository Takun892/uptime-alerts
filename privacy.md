# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 19, 2026

## Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how this personal infrastructure monitoring service collects, uses, and protects information related to SMS notifications generated by Uptime Kuma.

This service is operated for personal use and is not intended for commercial marketing, advertising, or customer communications.

---

## Information We Collect

The only personal information collected for SMS notifications is:

- Recipient name (optional)
- Mobile phone number

No additional personal information is collected through this service.

---

## How Information Is Used

Phone numbers are used solely to deliver automated infrastructure monitoring notifications, including but not limited to:

- Server availability alerts
- Service outage notifications
- Home Internet connectivity alerts
- CCTV monitoring notifications
- Other system health or operational alerts

Phone numbers are not used for marketing, advertising, or promotional messages.

---

## SMS Consent

Recipients are added only after providing their consent to receive SMS notifications.

**SMS consent is not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes.**

---

## Information Sharing

Recipient information is not sold, rented, traded, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Information is only used for the delivery of monitoring notifications generated by this service.

---

## Message Frequency

Message frequency varies depending on monitored system events.

Recipients may receive multiple alerts during service interruptions or extended outages.

---

## Carrier Charges

Message and data rates may apply depending on the recipient's mobile carrier and service plan.

---

## Opt-Out

Recipients may opt out of receiving SMS notifications at any time by replying **STOP** to any message or by requesting removal from the notification list.

---

## Assistance

Recipients may reply **HELP** to receive assistance or contact the account owner directly regarding these notifications.

---

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes to the monitoring service or applicable messaging requirements.

---

## Contact

Questions regarding this Privacy Policy or these notifications may be directed to the account owner.
