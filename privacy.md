# Privacy Policy

This notification service is used solely for personal infrastructure monitoring.

## Information Collected

Recipient phone numbers are stored only for the purpose of delivering monitoring alerts.

## Information Sharing

Phone numbers, SMS consent, and personal information are never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

## Message Frequency

Messages are sent only when monitoring events occur. Message frequency varies depending on system activity.

## Charges

Message and data rates may apply according to your mobile carrier.

## Contact

Recipients may contact the account owner at any time regarding these notifications.
