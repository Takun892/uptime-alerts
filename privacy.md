# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 19, 2026

## Overview

This website supports **Andrew Mack Uptime Alerts**, a private personal infrastructure monitoring service that delivers automated SMS notifications generated by Uptime Kuma.

The service is used exclusively for personal operational monitoring and is not intended for marketing, advertising, promotional messaging, or public customer communications.

---

## Information We Collect

To provide SMS notifications, the following information may be maintained:

- Recipient name, if provided
- Mobile phone number
- Recipient consent status

No additional personal information is intentionally collected through this service.

---

## How We Use Your Information

Recipient phone numbers are used solely to deliver automated personal infrastructure monitoring notifications, including:

- Server availability alerts
- Service outage notifications
- Home Internet connectivity alerts
- CCTV connectivity notifications
- Infrastructure health notifications
- Service recovery notifications

Phone numbers are never used for advertising, marketing, or promotional communications.

---

## SMS Consent

Recipients are added only after explicitly agreeing to receive automated SMS notifications from Andrew Mack Uptime Alerts.

Before a phone number is added, the recipient is informed about:

- The nature of the monitoring notifications
- Variable message frequency
- Possible message and data charges
- The ability to reply **STOP** to unsubscribe
- The ability to reply **HELP** for assistance

The full consent process and exact consent language are available on the [SMS Consent page](consent).

**SMS consent and mobile phone numbers are not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.**

---

## Information Sharing

Recipient information is never sold, rented, traded, or shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Information may be processed only as necessary to operate the monitoring service and deliver SMS notifications through the selected messaging provider.

---

## Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on monitored system events.

Recipients may receive multiple notifications during outages, service interruptions, status changes, or recovery events.

---

## Carrier Charges

Message and data rates may apply depending on the recipient's mobile carrier and service plan.

---

## Opt-Out

Recipients may stop receiving SMS notifications at any time by replying **STOP** to any message or by requesting removal from the notification list.

After opting out, no further monitoring notifications will be sent unless the recipient later requests to resubscribe.

---

## Assistance

Recipients may reply **HELP** for assistance or contact the account owner directly.

---

## Data Retention

Recipient phone numbers and consent records are retained only while the recipient remains authorized to receive notifications or as necessary to operate the service.

Recipient information may be removed when the recipient unsubscribes or is removed from the authorized notification list.

---

## Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes to the monitoring service or messaging requirements.
