# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 19, 2026

## Overview

This website supports a personal infrastructure monitoring service that delivers automated SMS notifications generated by Uptime Kuma.

The service is used exclusively for personal operational monitoring and is not intended for marketing, advertising, or commercial messaging.

---

## Information We Collect

To provide SMS notifications, the following information may be maintained:

- Recipient name (optional)
- Mobile phone number

No additional personal information is intentionally collected through this service.

---

## How We Use Your Information

Recipient phone numbers are used solely to deliver automated monitoring notifications, including but not limited to:

- Server availability alerts
- Service outage notifications
- Home Internet connectivity alerts
- CCTV monitoring notifications
- Infrastructure health notifications

Phone numbers are never used for advertising, marketing, or promotional communications.

---

## SMS Consent

Recipients are added only after providing their consent to receive SMS notifications.

**SMS consent and mobile phone numbers are not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.**

---

## Information Sharing

Recipient information is never sold, rented, traded, or disclosed to third parties for marketing purposes.

Information is used only as necessary to deliver monitoring notifications through the selected messaging provider.

---

## Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on monitored system events.

Recipients may receive multiple notifications during outages or service interruptions.

---

## Carrier Charges

Message and data rates may apply depending on the recipient's mobile carrier and service plan.

---

## Opt-Out

Recipients may stop receiving SMS notifications at any time by replying **STOP** to any message or by requesting removal from the notification list.

---

## Assistance

Recipients may reply **HELP** for assistance or contact the account owner directly.

---

## Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes to the monitoring service or messaging requirements.

---

## Contact

Questions regarding this Privacy Policy or the SMS notification service may be directed to the account owner.
